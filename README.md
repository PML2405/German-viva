# 🇩🇪 Deutsch Viva Guide

An interactive study guide for the **German for Beginners** oral exam (Viva Voce), based on the *Willkommen* 3rd Edition textbook.

Covers all **50 viva questions** with sample answers, English translations, and grammar tips — searchable and filterable by topic. Works on any device.

**Topics covered:** Identity · Studies · Language · Hobbies & Sports · Food & Drink · Family · Time & Plans · Dreams & More
